Giving back

March 20, 2021

Hi there ! This is a quick note about giving back.

I am trying to incorporate donations in my finance plan.

I like what (give well)[https://www.givewell.org] is doing.

They are studying charities to understand which charities have the biggest impact for one dollar invested. All their research is transparent, so you can follow along their reasoning. They even have one page when their list their failures in analysis, process etc and improvements.

You can find (here)[https://www.givewell.org/charities/top-charities], the updated list of the most impactful top charities. They created as well a Maximum Impact Fund to allocate money between all those charities.

They estimate that you can save a live for around $3,000 - $5,000. Imagine that you could save 50 lifes in a lifetime. This is not negligeable!

So here are my personal goals for year 2021:

  • help a life with a donation of around $3,000 - $5,000 this year
  • learn more about effecitve donations: read Banker to the poor, Dead aid
